Whether you're here for the slopes, the hiking trails, the alpine lakes, or simply the fresh mountain air — Alpenglück Tauplitz has you covered in every season.

Location

Tauplitz is a small alpine village in Styria, Austria, situated at around 900 m altitude. It is the gateway to the Tauplitzalm (1,650 m), a beautiful high plateau with ski slopes in winter and walking trails, mountain lakes, and wildlife in summer.

Nearest ski resort: Tauplitz–Bad Mitterndorf
Nearest town: Bad Mitterndorf (~5 km)
Nearest city: Liezen (~25 km)  |  Graz (~120 km)

Discover Tauplitz & the Ausseerland

Tauplitz sits in the Ausseerland–Salzkammergut, one of the most beautiful corners of Austria — a landscape of turquoise lakes, limestone peaks and storybook villages, much of it UNESCO World Heritage. Here is what you can reach from the apartment.

🏔️ Tauplitzalm & the Six Lakes

On your doorstep

The Tauplitzalm is one of the largest high plateaus in the Alps, lying between roughly 1,650 and 1,965 m. In summer the famous Six-Lakes Walk (6-Seen-Wanderung) winds past the Steirersee, Großsee, Schwarzensee, Tauplitzsee, Krukensee and the tiny Märchensee ("Fairytale Lake"). Reach the plateau by chairlift from the village or via the panoramic Alpine toll road.

Tip: the Six-Lakes Walk takes about 3–4 hours and suits reasonably fit families.

🛶 Grundlsee & the Three-Lakes Tour

~25 min

The largest lake in Styria, nicknamed the "Styrian Sea". Swim in its clear water, or take the classic Three-Lakes Tour by traditional wooden boat: Grundlsee → the mysterious Toplitzsee (wrapped in legends of sunken wartime treasure) → the remote Kammersee, source of the river Traun.

Tip: combine the boat tour with a lakeside lunch in Gössl.

🏘️ Hallstatt – UNESCO Heritage

~45 min

Perhaps the most photographed village in the world, clinging to a steep mountainside above its lake. Wander the tiny lanes, descend into the world's oldest salt mine, ride up to the Skywalk "Welterbeblick" for the postcard view, and visit the curious Bone House beside the church.

Tip: arrive early morning or late afternoon to beat the day-trip crowds.

🚠 Dachstein & Krippenstein

~40 min

Ride the cable car from Obertraun up to the Krippenstein and the thrilling 5 Fingers viewing platform jutting over a sheer drop. Explore the giant Dachstein Ice Cave and Mammoth Cave, or head for the glacier with its suspension bridge, "Stairway to Nothingness" and ice palace.

Tip: bring a warm layer — it stays cold in the ice caves all summer.

🌼 Bad Aussee & Altaussee

~20 min

Bad Aussee, the spa town at the geographic heart of Austria, bursts into colour each spring for the Narzissenfest (Daffodil Festival). Nearby Altaussee has one of the region's prettiest lakes, the dramatic Loser panoramic road, and a salt mine that famously hid looted masterpieces during WWII.

Tip: drive the Loser Panoramastraße for jaw-dropping views over the lakes.

🎿 The Kulm – Ski Flying Arena

~10 min

Just down the valley near Bad Mitterndorf stands the Kulm, one of the largest ski-flying hills on the planet, which periodically hosts the Ski Flying World Championships. Even out of season, the sheer scale of the structure is breathtaking.

Tip: check the events calendar — a flying competition is an unforgettable spectacle.

♨️ Bad Mitterndorf & GrimmingTherme

~5 min

Your nearest town for shops, cafés and restaurants — and home to the modern GrimmingTherme thermal spa. It is the perfect way to soothe tired muscles after a day on the mountain, all under the gaze of the Grimming, the "lonely giant" and signature peak of the region.

Tip: a rainy-day favourite — pools, saunas and relaxation areas.

🗺️ More to explore

Nearby

Pürgg, the "Cradle of Styria", a hilltop village with 12th-century frescoes • the turquoise Salza reservoir • the quiet swimming lake Ödensee • and neighbouring ski areas Riesneralm and Planneralm for more pistes and trails.

Tip: ask us for our personal favourites — happy to share local secrets.

Every season has its magic

❄️ Winter

  • Skiing & snowboarding on the Tauplitz (≈ 40 km of pistes)
  • Cross-country skiing & winter walking trails
  • Ski touring & snowshoeing across the plateau
  • Tobogganing fun for the whole family
  • Ski-flying spectacles at the Kulm
  • Thermal spa days at the GrimmingTherme

☀️ Summer

  • The Six-Lakes Walk on the Tauplitzalm
  • Swimming & boat tours on Grundlsee and Altaussee
  • Day trips to Hallstatt & the Dachstein
  • Mountain biking & road cycling
  • Via ferrata & alpine climbing
  • Salt mines, mountain huts and village festivals

Getting here

Tauplitz is a small alpine village in Styria, Austria, at around 900 m altitude, and the gateway to the Tauplitzalm plateau (up to 1,965 m).

Nearest town: Bad Mitterndorf (~5 km)
Nearest train station: Bad Mitterndorf–Heilbrunn / Stainach-Irdning
Nearest cities: Liezen (~25 km)  |  Graz (~120 km)  |  Salzburg (~110 km)

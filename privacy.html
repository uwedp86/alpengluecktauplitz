We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR – Regulation 2016/679).

1. Who is responsible for your data?

The data controller for this website is the private owner of Alpenglück Tauplitz. You can reach us at: alpenglueck.tauplitz@gmail.com

2. What data we collect — and what we do NOT collect

This website does not:

  • Use cookies of any kind (functional, analytical, or marketing).
  • Use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking tool.
  • Collect names, email addresses, or any personal data through a form.
  • Create user accounts or store login information.

Email contact: If you choose to contact us at the email address above, we will receive and process only the information you include in your message (name, email address, and any details you provide). This data is used solely to respond to your enquiry and arrange your booking. It is not shared with third parties and is deleted once the booking relationship has ended.

Server logs: GitHub Pages (the hosting service) may automatically log technical information such as IP addresses for security and stability purposes. This is governed by GitHub's own privacy policy: GitHub Privacy Statement.

3. External links

This website contains links to Booking.com and Airbnb. When you click these links you leave our website and are subject to the privacy policies of those platforms. We have no control over and accept no responsibility for their data practices.

4. Legal basis for processing (Art. 6 GDPR)

When you contact us by email, we process your personal data on the basis of Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (processing necessary for the performance of a contract or pre-contractual steps) and/or Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in responding to enquiries).

5. Data retention

Email correspondence is kept only as long as necessary to manage the booking and to comply with any applicable legal obligations (e.g. tax records), after which it is deleted.

6. Your rights under the GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you (Art. 15).
  • Rectify inaccurate data (Art. 16).
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten") (Art. 17).
  • Restrict processing (Art. 18).
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest (Art. 21).
  • Lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority in your country of residence. In Belgium: Gegevensbeschermingsautoriteit (GBA) .

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at alpenglueck.tauplitz@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version. As this website does not collect personal data proactively, changes are unlikely to affect you materially.
